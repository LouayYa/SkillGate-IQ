# Acme Corp Role-to-Certification Map

**Document ID:** LRN-MAP-002
**Owner:** Acme Corp Learning & Enablement Office
**Status:** Synthetic reference content for demonstration only.

## Purpose

Maps each engineering role to its primary and secondary certifications and the recommended study pattern. Use this to determine which certification a learner should target based on their role, and what a realistic study cadence looks like.

## Cloud Engineer

- **Primary certification:** ACP-CE1 (Acme Certified Cloud Engineer)
- **Secondary / next step:** ACP-SA4 (Acme Certified Solutions Architect)
- **Recommended study pattern:** 1–2 focused hours per weekday; one weekly review checkpoint. Target completion of ACP-CE1 within 4–6 weeks.

## DevOps Engineer

- **Primary certification:** ACP-DO2 (Acme Certified DevOps Professional)
- **Prerequisite reminder:** ACP-CE1 must be completed first.
- **Secondary / next step:** ACP-SA4
- **Recommended study pattern:** 1.5–2 focused hours per weekday; weekly checkpoint; emphasise hands-on pipeline labs. Target completion within 6–8 weeks.

## Data Engineer

- **Primary certification:** ACP-DE1 (Acme Certified Data Engineer)
- **Secondary / next step:** ACP-SA4
- **Recommended study pattern:** 1–2 focused hours per weekday; weekly checkpoint; alternate theory with data-pipeline labs. Target completion within 5–7 weeks.

## Solutions Architect

- **Primary certification:** ACP-SA4 (Acme Certified Solutions Architect)
- **Prerequisite reminder:** ACP-CE1 must be completed first.
- **Recommended study pattern:** 2 focused hours per weekday plus weekend case-study review; weekly checkpoint. Target completion within 8–10 weeks given the expert level.

## Sequencing rules

- A learner targeting ACP-DO2 or ACP-SA4 who has not completed ACP-CE1 should sequence ACP-CE1 first, or have its skills explicitly covered in early milestones.
- Higher-difficulty skills (Security Architecture, Batch & Stream Processing, Secure DevOps) should be scheduled after their foundational skills (Identity & Access Basics, Data Ingestion & Pipelines, CI/CD Pipelines).
